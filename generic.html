What We Offer

Following my father's cigar and vape shop success, I opened Cigar Boutique and Vape Love with the desire to provide only premium cigars, e-liquids, and vaping accessories all with excellent service to satisfy the smoking and vaping needs of the community. Having opened our doors back in 2013, we now enjoy a strong connection to the community as well as amazing relationship with our customers.

With a strict focus on anything smoke or vape, we bring an expansive selection of cigars of top quality, state of the art vaping accessories and premium e-liquids to not only bring quality to our customers, but a large selection from which to choose from.
